Accreditations and Ranking :
Tamralipta Mahavidyalaya is approved by AICTE, NAAC, and accredited by the University of Calcutta, Kolkata.

Course Offered :
Tamralipta Mahavidyalaya offers Degree Courses Bachelor of Education [B.Ed], Bachelor of Arts- BA (Hons. & General), Bachelor of Science [B.Sc] {Hons. & General} Bachelor of Commerce [B.Com] (Accountancy), B.Ed. in Bengali, History, Sanskrit, MA, M.Sc in Zoology.
Infrastructure and facilities:
Library:

Ours is a rich library stocked with more than 29,500 books, 12 journals, 16 periodicals, and a large no. of CDs and DVDs. Modern devices of classification, cataloging, and indexing have been adopted. The students can avail of the Reading, Borrowing, and Book-Bank facilities. The library is located on the 1St floor of the administrative building and Reading Room is attached to the library. The library remains open on all working days except on Sundays and Holidays. Even during recess the students and the teachers can avail the library facilities. Online Public Access Catalogue (OPAC) searching, Internet surfing, and E-mail facilities are also provided to the readers through a broadband connection.

Laboratories:

The college has adequate laboratory facilities for all Lab-based subjects (both Honours and General) which provide adequate and intensive practical coaching to the students of the Science section. Moreover, the Lab-based departments are equipped with computer and internet facilities.

Museum:

Tamluk being a place of historical interest, the college feels it obligatory to have a museum of its own. The college museum, though small, possesses some valuable antiquities, relics, and archaeological finds.

Hostel:

The College has three hostels, one for boys, and the other two are for girls students. They are well governed by the teacher and superintendents. The admission is made strictly on the basis of distance from the student’s residence. Priorities are given to S.C., S.T., and handicapped students. The boys’ hostel has an intake capacity of 60 students and the two girls’ hostel can afford 42 seats. No married student is allowed to stay in the hostel. Any unsuccessful candidate in any university examination will be given one more chance to overstay in the hostel. The rules and regulations laid by the Hostel Committee are obligatory to the inmates of the hostel.

Canteen:

There is a Cheap Canteen in the college that caters food to the students at a subsidized rate.

Staff Quarters:

The College has eight full-fledged quarters for the staff of the College including Teaching and Non-teaching.

Cycle Stand:

There is a large cycle stand.

Auditorium:

The College has a large Auditorium to organize seminar and other cultural program.

Gymnasium:

A big hall is used as Gymnasium where various modern equipments for indoor physical training are available. Boys’ and Girls’ Common Rooms: Two separate common rooms with necessary facilities for the male and female students of the college are available.

Mission Our mission is as follows: Equipping students with appropriate knowledge to cope with realities of life at the local, national, and global level,· Produce genuine students demonstrating knowledge, attitude, and skills in an integrated manner.

Vision:
To make this institution a ‘Centre of Excellence’ by equipping all students with requisite knowledge, basic indigenous values and 21st century skills in an inclusive setup for their personal fulfillment and social contribution
